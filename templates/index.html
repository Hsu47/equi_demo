PDF Extraction — Live Parser

LPs receive hundreds of GP reports as PDFs each quarter. Manual data entry is slow and error-prone — the real danger isn't "can't parse", it's "parsed wrong but looks right".

Our parser extracts, cross-validates, and tells you how much to trust the result. Click below to see it live on a real LP statement.  ·  view source PDF ↗
